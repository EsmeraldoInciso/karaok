# KaraOK Monetization Plan — Filipino Market

## Why This Market Works

Karaoke is deeply embedded in Filipino culture — from videoke machines in every barangay to family gatherings and inuman sessions. KaraOK solves a real pain point: **no need to rent a machine, no coins, just a phone and a screen.**

---

## Pricing Tiers

### Tier 1: Free (Current)

Keep the free tier generous to drive adoption:

- Create/join sessions
- Queue songs
- Basic search
- 24-hour session limit
- Max ~10 participants per session

### Tier 2: KaraOK Premium (₱149/month or ₱1,299/year)

Target: Regular users, friend groups, family households

| Feature                    | Why it sells                                    |
| -------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| **Unlimited participants** | Big Filipino families, barkada gatherings        |
| **Extended sessions (72h)**| Weekend-long celebrations, fiestas               |
| **Priority queue**         | Premium users' songs move up                     |
| **No ads**                 | Clean experience                                 |
| **Song history**           | "What was that song from last inuman?"           |
| **Custom playlists**       | Save favorites for next session                  |

**Payment:** GCash and Maya integration (most Filipinos don't have credit cards). Use **PayMongo** — Philippine payment gateway that supports GCash, Maya, card, and GrabPay.

### Tier 3: KaraOK Business (₱499/month)

Target: Bars, restos, KTV lounges, events companies

| Feature                    | Why it sells                                    |
| -------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| **Branded sessions**       | Show bar logo/name on player screen              |
| **Permanent session rooms**| No need to recreate codes nightly                |
| **Queue moderation**       | Staff can reorder/remove songs                   |
| **Multiple screens**       | Sync one session to 2+ TVs                       |
| **Analytics dashboard**    | Popular songs, peak hours, customer count         |
| **No "KaraOK" branding**  | White-label the player view                      |

## Revenue Stream 2: Ads (Free Tier)

- **Between-song ads** — 5-second splash screen ad between songs (not during)
- **Sponsored search results** — promote music videos from Filipino artists (work with record labels like Star Music, Viva Records)
- **Banner ads** on the remote/queue view
- Use **Google AdSense** initially, then direct ad deals with local brands

Target CPM for PH market: ₱50–150 (~$1–3)

---

## Revenue Stream 3: Virtual Tips / "Patok" System

Filipino culture loves friendly competition and hype:

| Feature              | How it works                                              |
| -------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| **"Patok" reactions**| Participants tap to give stars during a performance        |
| **Tip the singer**   | Send ₱10–50 virtual tips via GCash (KaraOK takes 15% cut)|
| **Leaderboard**      | Most patok'd singer of the night                          |
| **Session MVP badge**| Bragging rights                                           |

This taps into the Filipino love of **pakikipagsaya** (communal fun) and friendly competition.

## Revenue Stream 4: Events & Partnerships

### Corporate/Event packages (₱2,999–9,999 one-time)

- Company team buildings
- Wedding receptions
- Fiesta entertainment
- University events
- Branded session with logo, custom playlist, dedicated support

### Partnerships

- **Smart/Globe** — bundle KaraOK Premium with data plans
- **GCash/Maya** — in-app promotions, cashback on premium
- **San Miguel / Tanduay** — sponsored sessions ("Tanduay Karaoke Night")
- **Local bars/restos** — affiliate program, refer customers for commission

---

## Revenue Stream 5: Song Request Marketplace

- **Filipino indie artists** pay to have their songs promoted in search results
- **Record labels** sponsor "Featured Artist" playlists
- Users can request specific hard-to-find karaoke tracks — crowdsourced demand signals valuable to labels

## Go-to-Market Strategy for PH

### Phase 1: Grassroots (Month 1–3)

- Launch free on social media — TikTok/Reels demos of KaraOK at parties
- Target university students (barkada sessions)
- Facebook Groups: "Karaoke Lovers PH", "Inuman Sessions"
- Tagline: *"Libre lang, walang barya needed"* (It's free, no coins needed)

### Phase 2: Viral Growth (Month 3–6)

- "KaraOK Challenge" on TikTok — best karaoke performance using the app
- Partner with Filipino YouTubers/streamers
- Referral program: invite 5 friends → 1 month free Premium

### Phase 3: Monetize (Month 6+)

- Roll out Premium tier
- Approach bars/KTV in Metro Manila for Business tier pilots
- Enable GCash tipping
- Start between-song ads for free users

---

## Pricing Rationale

- **₱149/month** (~$2.70) — affordable for Filipino middle class, cheaper than 1 hour of KTV rental
- **₱499/month** for businesses — cheaper than maintaining a physical videoke machine (₱15,000–30,000 purchase + song updates)
- Compare: Spotify PH is ₱149/month, Netflix is ₱149–549 — this sits comfortably in that range
