Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

1. Introduction

KaraOK ("we", "our", "us") is a web-based karaoke session platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use KaraOK:

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address and display name. If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email, and profile picture from Google.
  • Session Data: When you create or join a karaoke session, we store session codes, song queue data, and participant information.
  • Usage Data: We may collect basic usage information such as timestamps of when sessions are created or joined.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the karaoke session service
  • To authenticate your identity and manage your account
  • To display your name to other participants in a session
  • To maintain and improve the service

4. Third-Party Services

KaraOK uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication, database, and hosting. See Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • YouTube API: To search and play karaoke videos. Your use of YouTube content is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google reCAPTCHA: To protect against bots and abuse. See Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase servers. We use Firebase Authentication and Firestore Security Rules to protect your data. Only authenticated users can access session data, and only session participants can view queue information.

6. Data Retention

Session data (queues, participants) is retained while a session is active and may be retained for a reasonable period after a session ends. Account data is retained as long as your account exists. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

8. Children's Privacy

KaraOK is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at esmeraldoinciso@gmail.com.
